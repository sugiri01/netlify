Goals and Objectives

Short-term Goals:

  • Launch and promote AI and Data Science-related courses through EdAthena.
  • Highlight the innovative capabilities of Qual.AI.
  • Showcase EcoLearn.AI for affordable education with a clear path to employment.
  • Enroll 500 students and secure partnerships with institutions for Qual.AI and EcoLearn.AI.

Long-term Goals:

  • Position EdAthena as the leading provider of AI and Data Science education in India.
  • Expand EdAthena and EcoLearn.AI internationally.
  • Ensure Synergy.AI and Qual.AI become tools for collaboration between academia and industry.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths:

  • Expertise in AI and Data Science with Qual.AI.
  • EcoLearn.AI offers affordable, accessible education linked to employment.
  • Strong alignment with industry needs for employment-focused learning.

Weaknesses:

  • Limited brand awareness and marketing resources.
  • Small team size affecting the ability to scale.

Opportunities:

  • Growing demand for AI and Data Science education.
  • Collaboration with institutions for real-world AI projects via EcoLearn.AI.

Threats:

  • Increasing competition from established platforms.
  • Rapid advancements in technology requiring continuous updates.

Unique Selling Point (USP)

For EdAthena:

  • Offering accessible, affordable AI and Data Science courses.
  • Integration with Qual.AI and EcoLearn.AI for personalized learning and employability.

For Qual.AI:

  • First AI-powered Adaptive Learning Management System.
  • Personalizes learning based on student performance.

For EcoLearn.AI:

  • Provides affordable education with a path to real-world employment.
  • Focus on employability and bridging the gap between education and job readiness.

For Synergy.AI:

  • Acts as a company interaction tool to bridge the industry-academia gap.
  • Facilitates collaboration for real-world projects between businesses and students.
